Tacos Al Pastor

Ingredients

  • 5 lb boneless pork shoulder
  • 3 tablespoons achiote paste
  • 2 tablespoons guajillo chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon cumin
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon pepper
  • 3/4 cup white vinegar
  • 1 cup pineapple juice
  • 1 pineapple, skinned and sliced into 1-inch (2 cm) rounds
  • 1 wooden skweer to fit oven

Directions

  1. Slice the pork shoulder into about 1-centimeter slices and combine with dry and wet ingredients in bowl. Leave in fridge for 2 hours.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F. Line baking sheet with parchment paper or aluminum foil.
  3. Take wooden skweer and push in middle of two pineapple slices.
  4. Take pork slices out of fridge and start stacking on top of each other on the skweer.
  5. Bake for 1 1/2 hours until pork is slightly charred outside and deep red. Rest meat for 10 minutes.
  6. Carve off thin slices of pork and roasted pineapple and assemble with cooked tortillas.

Air-Fried Chicken Sandwhich

Ingredients

  • 2 Boneless, Skinless Chicken Breasts
  • 1 cup pickle juice
  • 1/2 cup of buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • cooking oil

Directions

  1. Place thawed out chicken breasts in a large bowl with pickle juice and buttermilk. Refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  2. Combine flower and spices in a large bowl.
  3. Spray air fryer basket with cooking oil.
  4. Remove chicken from marinade and dredge in flour mix.
  5. Spray top with cooking oil and Air Fry for 15 minutes.
  6. Open air fryer and flip chicken cooking for an additional 5 minutes until the chicken is crisp.
  7. Remove chicken, assemble sandwhich, and enjoy.

Revotillio de Huevos

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3/4 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 large onion
  • 8 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 tablespoon of minced parsley

Directions

  1. Heat a tablespoon of oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add tomato, bell pepper, onion, and cook and stir until the vegetables are cooked through.
  2. Add the eggs, stir until the eggs set. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with parsley.
  3. Serve and enjoy.
